Frequently Asked Questions

Common technical questions regarding system integration, longevity, and security.

What chemical composition does Stackly use?

By default, Stackly uses Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) chemistry due to its thermal stability, low cycle degradation, and high round-trip efficiency. For long-duration storage (8+ hours), we offer Vanadium Redox Flow systems.

How does liquid cooling maximize battery lifetime?

Batteries degrade exponentially under excessive temperatures. Liquid glycol channels absorb cell heat much faster than HVAC air blowers, ensuring that individual cells remain inside the narrow 20-28°C range required for 20-year lifetimes.

Can Stackly systems black-start a microgrid?

Yes. Our utility-scale bi-directional smart inverters support grid-forming capabilities. In the event of a total regional blackout, Stackly BESS systems can safely re-energize local lines and synchronize local solar generation.
