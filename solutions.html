Utility Operations

Utility-Scale BESS (PowerCube Pro)

Stackly PowerCube Pro delivers containerized battery capacities from 2MWh to 10MWh, engineered for utilities, solar farms, and regional grid operators.

  • Modular Expansion: Interlink dozens of container configurations to support gigawatt-level capacities.
  • Grid Compliance: Active power-frequency support, inertia emulation, and black-start capabilities.
  • Liquid Cooled: Active glycol channels minimize degradation spikes across individual battery racks.

Commercial Operations

Commercial & Industrial Battery Hubs

Our commercial product configurations enable warehouses, hospitals, microgrids, and data centers to avoid volatile electric tariffs.

  • Demand Peak Shaving: Automatically discharges battery during the building's highest demand intervals.
  • Uninterrupted Power (UPS): Seamless sub-cycle transition (< 8ms) in the event of grid blackouts.
  • Microgrid Orchestration: Pairs directly with on-site solar carports and local diesel backups.
