About Stackly

Accelerating the Global Carbon-Free Future

Stackly was founded with a singular mission: to eliminate renewable energy curtailment and stabilize global electricity grids through modern, grid-scale energy storage architectures.

Our History

Our Growth Milestones

From a silicon valley lab to managing gigawatts of power capacity worldwide.

2018

Stackly Founded

Established by a team of energy storage experts and software engineers in Oregon to tackle electrochemical longevity constraints.

2020

First Containerized BESS

Launched the first 2MW grid-interactive lithium iron phosphate storage containers with integrated liquid thermal management.

2023

AI EMS Software Release

Released Stackly EMS Core, utilizing predictive machine learning models to maximize grid arbitrage and battery round-trip yield.

2026

5.4 GW Power Benchmark

Successfully deployed and managed over 5.4GW of active storage projects across 22 countries, enabling major clean utility grids.
